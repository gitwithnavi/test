About Safe Security

[email protected]

Founded in 2012 and headquartered in Palo Alto, Safe Security is a global leader in Enterprise Cybersecurity and Digital Business Risk Quantification (CRQ). Incubated in IIT Bombay and backed by Cisco's former Chairman and CEO John Chambers, it protects multiple Fortune 500 companies and governments around the world with its SAFE platform. Safe Security was awarded ‘Emerging Vendor of the Year 2018' by Frost & Sullivan, recognized as a 'Leading Pure-Play VAPT Vendor' by Gartner’s Market Guide in 2019, and awarded “Morgan Stanley CTO Innovation Award 2019”.
